About me

My name is Filip Zynwala. I was born and I live in Wroclaw. I graduated from the Wrocław University of Technology in the field of Electronics and Telecommunications. I've been interested in web programming since the summer of 2019, when I took part in the 4th edition of CodersCamp. This course, led by the CodersCrew association, gave me a solid foundation and ignited a passion for further development in this field. After completing the course, I got involved in the next edition of CodersCamp (5th edition), where I became a Mentor and led a group of 8 people.

I am currently looking for my first job as a Junior Frontend Developer. I will also gladly undertake any site as a freelancer. You can contact me by sending an email or via social media.
